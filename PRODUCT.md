# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Local diners in the city choosing where to eat for a special occasion, a family gathering, or a business lunch, who specifically want halal Central Asian / Caucasian cuisine served without compromise on atmosphere. Secondary: people ordering delivery or calling ahead for a phone reservation. Context: often browsing on mobile, comparing a handful of restaurants before calling.

## Product Purpose

A marketing site for **Оскар**, a halal restaurant, that makes the food and the room look as good in photos as the best dining rooms in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, and converts that impression into a phone call or WhatsApp message to reserve a table or order delivery. Success is a visitor picking up the phone.

## Positioning

Halal dining with the polish of the Gulf's best restaurants — authentic tandyr, shashlik, plov, and Caucasian dishes served in a room that takes itself as seriously as its five-star inspirations.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: "Заказать столик" (call / WhatsApp reservation)
- Secondary CTA: "Посмотреть меню"
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: this is a serious, beautiful halal restaurant, not a canteen.
- Belief ladder: the craft of the page signals a premium room → the food photography proves the kitchen delivers → real Yandex ratings/reviews confirm other guests agree → reserving is one tap away (call or WhatsApp).
- Proof on hand: no written testimonials supplied. Reviews section links out to the restaurant's Yandex Maps listing rather than inventing quotes.

## Brand Personality

Восточная роскошь — warm, confident, unhurried luxury. Ornamental but not kitsch: Islamic geometric motifs, gold linework, deep emerald, soft directional light. Proud of being halal (badge, not caveat).

## Anti-references

Not a fast-casual/canteen look (bright primary colors, laminated menu photography, harsh flash lighting). Not generic SaaS-brand gradients or stock "Middle Eastern" clip-art patterns.

## Design Principles

- Let the real food photography carry the page — no stock imagery, no decorative filler standing in for a dish.
- Ornament frames content, it never competes with it: geometric motifs and gold linework mark thresholds (section edges, cards, dividers), not every surface.
- Every primary action is one tap from a phone: call or WhatsApp, never a dead-end form.
- Halal is stated with quiet confidence, not as a small disclaimer.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all body text (dark emerald body backgrounds need ink-light text, not muted gold-gray). Full reduced-motion fallback for ornamental motion. Tap targets sized for one-handed phone use, since most visitors will be on mobile deciding whether to call.
